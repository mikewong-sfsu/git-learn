Instructions to Parents

Before starting to answer the questions please read and understand this section. If you need any clarification please ask.

We are looking for specific pattern of visual difficulties using the Higher Visual Function Question Inventory (HVFQI) to determine how your brain processes visual images sent by the eye and helps you understand your visual environment.

The pictures of what you see through your eyes are sent to your brain where they are made into a mental copy of the scene. This copy is used by the brain to guide your movements and to find and recognise things. Cerebral visual impairment (CVI) affects this process. So, it can lead to any combination or pattern of difficulties with guiding your movements, and finding and recognizing people, objects and routes. A good term for what this HVFQI does is to find out about your brain-based visually guided behaviour.

We want to find out what challenges you now have through this HVFQI. Please let us also know if these challenges are new or longstanding. We also need to find out if they are getting better, or if you are adapting to them.

In addition, do let us know of any difficulties elicited from these questions that you feel are not wholly related to your visual perception and may be due to any other factor.

Your answers help us to understand the range, nature and severity of visual difficulties caused by CVI, but they do not give us all the information needed to make a diagnosis of CVI. A diagnosis of CVI is based on putting together multiple factors determined form a detailed history, eye examination, this question inventory and other examinations and tests.
